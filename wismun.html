What is MUN?

MUN stands for Model United Nations. It is a simulation platform of United Nations Conferences. It helps students/delegates to learn about International Issues and Global Crisis.

WhY TO DO MUN?

From worldy knowledge to public speaking, MUN experience is going to help you throught out your professional life. According to us our top 3 reasons to do MUN(s) are:
1. Increase Knowledge It's an enjoyment way of finding out about the world. In this era of globalization, being globally aware is more important than ever. Likewise, having some good times makes it simpler to get the hang of something and bound to stick
2. Public Speaking It develops leadership skills. MUN is an exercise in research, public speaking, and teamwork. These are skills that you will need throughout your career, and MUN gives you a chance to practice them while you’re a student.
3. Friend Circle You can leverage your MUN experience and network to get into college and find jobs. Admissions officers and job interviewers look for examples of leadership, and your experiences as a delegate or conference organizer will be good examples. You will also develop a network of alumni from your MUN club and people you’ve met at conferences..
